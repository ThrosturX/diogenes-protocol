Templates

How to use templates

  • Pick the smallest template that matches the request.
  • Fill in identifiers and dates first; then paste facts from your packet.
  • Keep outbound text boring: factual, bounded, deadline-aware.

MVP set:

  • GDPR DSAR (EU/EEA)
  • EU261 rerouting / reimbursement request
  • Retention hold / preservation notice
  • Account deletion request
  • Marketing opt-out
  • EU261 expenses appendix
  • GDPR DSAR follow-up (minimal ID)
  • Rectification request
  • EU261 compensation request
  • Ticketing mismatch / denied boarding
